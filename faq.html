Chapter 06

Frequently
Asked Things.

Answered without a PR team reviewing them first. You'll notice the difference.

Is this a rug? +
LP is burned. Mint is revoked. We have no mechanism to rug you that differs from any other token. Could the price go to zero? Yes — that's different from a rug. A rug requires intent. We have the opposite intent, which is this website, this philosophy, and a genuine attempt at something real. DYOR. Always. This is not financial advice.
When moon? +
We appreciate the classic. The answer: we don't know. Anyone who tells you otherwise with conviction is either wrong or selling something. What we can say: the community is real, the narrative is sticky, and good memes have always had market cap. Culture follows quality — eventually, sometimes, for the right ones. We think we're one. You decide if you agree.
Are you working with any KOLs or influencers? +
No. Hard no. KOL culture is how you get a 30-second pump followed by 300% bags and a Telegram full of people who were paid to be interested in you. If an influencer posts about $LATE, it's because they found it and thought it was good. We can't stop that — and honestly hope it happens organically. We just won't pay for performance.
What's the utility? +
The utility is cultural. The utility is identity. The utility is that when you say "I hold $LATE" in crypto spaces, people understand you immediately without further explanation. We're not building a DEX. We're not creating a "LATE-fi" ecosystem or a staking mechanism nobody uses. We're creating something people actually want to be part of. That's harder to build and more durable than fake utility.
Isn't buying $LATE just proving you're late to buying $LATE? +
Yes. That's the bit. You're always late to $LATE. The coin exists in a state of permanent temporal irony. You're either early and don't know it, or late and completely correct about yourself. Either way, you're exactly where you belong.
Do you have a team? Are they doxxed? +
There's a small team of people who believe in this. Pseudonymous, like most of crypto. We'd argue the token structure — burned LP, revoked mint, zero team allocation — does more to protect you than a LinkedIn profile. Actions over identity. That's the standard we hold ourselves to. Check the contract. That's the real doxx.
Why should I join the Telegram? +
Because it's the only crypto Telegram where people are actually funny, the conversation is real, and nobody will tell you to "buy the dip" while simultaneously dumping on you. It's also where updates happen first. Yes, this is a soft sell. We know. We're doing it anyway.
Is it too late to buy? +
That is literally the name of the coin. We cannot answer this without being part of the answer. What we can say: it's not financial advice, it's always too late for something, and it might also be earlier than you think. The paradox is the point. You already know the answer. You just haven't decided yet.

Still have questions?
That's actually a good sign.

The people who ask questions before buying are exactly who we want here. Come to Telegram. Ask them there. Someone will have a real answer, or a good enough joke that you'll forget you asked.
