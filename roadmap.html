Chapter 05

A roadmap.
Italicized
for irony.

Read this first. This is not a promise. This is not a legally binding document. This is a vibe document, written by people who are aware that crypto roadmaps are written by teams who have no idea what will happen next Tuesday — and presented as quarterly milestones. We refuse to lie to you in that particular way. What follows is what we actually intend to do, with the full caveat that intention and outcome are famously different things. If you're new here: welcome, we're glad you found us.
