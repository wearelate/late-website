Chapter 02

What is $LATE,
exactly?

Depends who you ask. Here's our answer — and we're not going to dress it up.

🟣
A Solana Meme Coin
Yes, technically. Fast finality, low fees, the whole ecosystem. But calling $LATE "just a meme coin" is like calling a mirror "just glass." Technically accurate. Spiritually insufficient.
🧠
A Cultural Object
$LATE documents the crypto experience that nobody admits: the missed entries, the late buys, the retrace that never came. It takes the most common emotion in crypto — regret — and makes it the brand.
🚫
Not a Promise
We will not promise a 100M MC. We will not create fake utilities. We will not hire KOLs to post "NGL this feels different 👀" while holding bags you paid for. That ship sailed. And you missed it.
🪞
A Self-Aware Paradox
The coin about being late might be the one you're actually early to. Or late to, again. The irony compounds. The bags don't always. That's the point. That's the joke. That's also not entirely a joke.
